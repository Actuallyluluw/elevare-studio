Who Are We?

>Elevare is a creative studio powered by emerging artists. Based in Aotearoa, we’re focused on digital experimentation, collaboration, and building space for new ideas to take shape.

Our Studios

Elevare Studio is a creative space dedicated to fostering emerging voices and bold ideas. Our studio is designed to be flexible, welcoming, and equipped for both digital and traditional practices — from illustration and painting to design and digital installation work.

We offer a range of afforable hire options, including short-term use for workshops, pop-ups, and as well as long- term shared studio space for artists and designers looking to work in a collaborative environment. Whether you’re hosting a class, installing an exhibition, or just need a space to build your next project, Elevare is a space made for making.

Our Exhibition Spaces

Discover a space designed for creativity, connection, and storytelling. Our exhibition areas are available for hire and offer the perfect backdrop for showcasing art, design, photography, installations, and interactive experiences. Whether you're an emerging artist or an established curator, our flexible, professional environment supports a wide range of creative practices.

With customizable layouts, access to lighting and display tools, and support from our in-house team, our space empowers you to bring your vision to life. Get in touch to learn more about our hire packages, availability, and how we can help make your next exhibition a success.
