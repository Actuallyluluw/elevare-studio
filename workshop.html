Life Drawing Workshop

Curated by Elevare Studio
Featuring works by emerging Aotearoa artists

Led by multidisciplinary artist Emille Stone, this immersive life drawing workshop offers a welcoming space to explore the human form through observational drawing. Known for her sensitive approach and intuitive mark-making, Emille guides participants through a range of exercises designed to loosen the hand, build confidence, and deepen understanding of anatomy, gesture, and proportion.
