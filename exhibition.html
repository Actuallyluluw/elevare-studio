CTRL // View

Curated by Elevare Studio
Featuring works by emerging Aotearoa artists

CTRL // View explores the tension between control and perception in our digital and physical worlds. This exhibition invites viewers to navigate the blurred boundaries between traditional and digital media, challenging how we interpret what we see and who controls the lens. Featuring work that manipulates software, glitches, photography, illustration, and interface, CTRL // View reframes our relationship with tools and visibility — asking: when we view, are we in control, or are we being controlled?
