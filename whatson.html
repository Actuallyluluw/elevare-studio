Open Call - Human Software

Elevare Studio invites artists to submit work for Human Software, an upcoming exhibition exploring the tension and harmony between digital tools and human touch. In an age where creativity flows through tablets, code, paintbrushes, and raw emotion alike.

We’re seeking work that blurs the line between analogue and digital — whether that’s through mixed media, 3D Models, animation layered over traditional sketches, or handmade processes mimicking digital aesthetics. This is a space for artists who embody both craft and code, process and emotion.

Submissions close: August 2nd
Mediums accepted: All forms
